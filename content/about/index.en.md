## About me

I'm a software architect, iSAQB-accredited trainer, and author.

I help teams design software systems that stay adaptable under real-world pressure. My focus is on distributed and agentic systems, architecture decision-making, and the trade-offs that determine whether systems evolve or become brittle.

My work spans three areas:

- Consulting: Architecture reviews, decision support, and system design for long-term adaptability.
- Training: Advanced architecture workshops for architects and senior engineers.
- Writing & speaking: Essays and talks on adaptive systems, AI, and engineering strategy.

If you want to build software that survives change, feel free to reach out.
